# "What's Inside The Lab" — Screen Recording Brief

**Purpose:** A short walkthrough video embedded at the top of the `/community` page to show prospective members exactly what they get before they click "Join The Lab." This is the single highest-converting element you can add to that page.

**Length:** 60–90 seconds. No longer.

**Where it goes:** Embedded on the `/community` page, above the pricing cards. (I'll add the embed once you upload it to YouTube.)

---

## What to Record

You are doing a quick screen tour of The Lab platform. Open your browser, log in to The Lab, and record your screen while you narrate. No editing needed — one take is fine.

**Suggested script / flow (read loosely, don't memorise):**

> "Hey — quick tour of what's inside The Lab. This is where everything lives."

1. **Show the home feed / community wall** (5 sec)
   > "This is the community — members post questions, share wins, and get feedback here every day."

2. **Click into the Courses tab** (10 sec)
   > "Over here are the courses — Google Ads, AI + SEO, GA4. All included in your membership."

3. **Click into the Thursday Q&A calendar or events tab** (10 sec)
   > "Every Thursday we do a live Q&A — you bring your real campaigns and we look at them together. It's recorded so you can watch back."

4. **Show one course module briefly** (10 sec)
   > "The lessons are short and practical — we skip the theory and go straight to what you actually do in the platform."

5. **Back to home, show the price** (5 sec)
   > "All of this is $29 a month. No contracts, cancel any time."

6. **Close with the CTA** (5 sec)
   > "If you want in, the link is right below this video."

---

## Recording Tips

- Use **Loom** (free at loom.com) — it records your screen and face at the same time. No editing required.
- Record at **1080p** if possible.
- You do **not** need to show your face — screen only is fine.
- Speak naturally. Imperfect is better than polished here — it builds trust.
- Upload to the **KnowHow Marketing Lab YouTube channel** as **Unlisted** (not public) so it doesn't appear in search but can be embedded on the site.

## Why This Works

Visitors who watch a "what's inside" video before a purchase decision convert at **2–3× the rate** of those who don't. It removes the biggest objection — "I don't know what I'm paying for" — before they even reach the price.
