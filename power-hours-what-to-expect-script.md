# Power Hours - "What to Expect" Video Script

**Format:** Short explainer, 60-90 seconds  
**Tone:** Warm, direct, no fluff - sounds like Pip talking to a business owner  
**Use:** Embed on /power-hours page above the registration CTA

---

## SCRIPT

---

**[OPEN - Pip to camera, casual setting]**

"Every Tuesday, I run a free live session called the Power Hour.

And I want to show you exactly what happens - because it's different from most marketing webinars you've been to.

There are no slides. No pitch deck. No hour of theory before you get to the useful bit.

You show up, you bring a real question about your website, your Google Ads, or your SEO - and we work through it live, on screen, together.

Last week someone asked why their Google Ads clicks weren't converting. We pulled up their account, looked at the data, and found the problem in about four minutes. That's the kind of session this is.

It's every Tuesday. It's free. You don't need to prepare anything - just bring one thing you want to get better at.

If you've got a question you've been sitting on, this is the place to bring it.

Register below and I'll see you Tuesday."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Length target:** 75-90 seconds
- **Setting:** Casual - at a desk or standing, not in front of a slide deck
- **B-roll suggestions:** Screen recording clip of a live session (blurred if needed), someone typing a question in chat, Pip reviewing a website or Google Ads account
- **End card:** "Register for free - link below" with the GHL calendar link
- **Thumbnail text:** "What actually happens in a Power Hour" or "Free live marketing help, every Tuesday"
