<idea>
**Design Movement**: Technical Precision / Data-Lab Aesthetic
**Core Principles**:
1. Dark navy + electric amber — authority meets energy
2. Asymmetric grid with intentional data-table motifs
3. Content-first hierarchy: video library as the centrepiece
4. Conversion architecture: every section has a visible next step

**Color Philosophy**: Deep navy (#0D1B2A) as the base signals expertise and trust. Electric amber (#E98C28) — the brand's own orange — acts as the conversion trigger on every CTA. Teal (#318599) is used for secondary accents and category tags. The palette says "serious professionals who get results."

**Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric split-screen hero (text left, video right). Video library uses a masonry/grid layout with category filter tabs. Sticky sidebar CTA on scroll. Sections separated by diagonal cuts rather than flat dividers.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Glowing amber CTA buttons with subtle pulse animation
2. Video cards with teal category badges and hover lift effect
3. "Lab" motif: subtle hexagonal grid texture in backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy**: Every hover reveals value — video cards show description on hover, CTA buttons glow. Scroll-triggered entrance animations for sections. Sticky nav with progress indicator.

**Animation**: Fade-up on scroll for content blocks. Video card hover: scale(1.03) + shadow lift. CTA button: pulse glow on idle. Hero text: staggered word entrance.

**Typography System**:
- Display: Space Grotesk Bold 700 — technical, modern, distinctive
- Body: DM Sans Regular 400 — clean, readable
- Accent/Labels: Space Mono — data/lab feel for category tags
</idea>

## Selected Design: Option 1 — Technical Precision / Data-Lab Aesthetic

**Rationale**: This approach best matches the brand's identity as a data-driven SEO and Google Ads education lab. The dark navy + amber palette mirrors the existing YouTube channel banner. The asymmetric layout and video-library-as-centrepiece structure directly serves the conversion goal of turning YouTube viewers into course and community members.
