**Content ratio:** 10 value posts for every 1 promotional post.
**Tone:** Conversational, practical, no jargon. Write like you're talking to a smart friend who doesn't know marketing.

## PART 6 — ENGAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

### The 3-Post Rule for New Members
When someone joins, within 48 hours:
1. **Welcome them personally** — reply to their membership answers or tag them in the welcome post
2. **Ask them one question** — based on their membership answers (e.g., "You mentioned your biggest challenge is getting found on Google — have you set up Google Search Console yet?")
3. **Point them to one resource** — a relevant post, the Power Hours registration, or a YouTube video

### What to Do When Someone Asks a Question
- Answer it publicly (not just in DMs) — other members benefit
- If it's a big question, say "Great question — I'm covering this at Power Hours this Tuesday, come and we'll go through it live"
- Tag other members who might have experience with the same issue

### Signs the Group is Working
- Members answering each other's questions (not just you)
- People sharing wins unprompted on Wednesdays
- Questions coming in before Tuesday's Power Hours
- New members introducing themselves without being asked

### Signs the Group Needs Attention
- Only you are posting
- Posts get views but no comments
- Members are not converting to Power Hours registrants

**Fix:** Go back to basics — post a direct question, reply to every comment personally, and DM the 5 most active members to ask what they want more of.
